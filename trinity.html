Trinity University

Trinity University is located in San Antonio, Texas. It was originally founded in 1869, 150 years ago. It currently has an enrollment of about 2,480 students and is ranked at sixty-one on "College Consensus" Best Colleges and Universities in the Nation list.


Photo courtesy of Trinity University

Located on 125 acres near the heart of San Antonio, Trinity's campus is known for its red brick buildings and beautiful live oaks.

The features about Trinity that I appreciate most are the small community, excellent professors, and beautiful campus.

I chose Trinity University because of it's beautiful campus and small community. I also liked that I would have the opportunity to participate in undergraduate research.
